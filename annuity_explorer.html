Annuity & Growing Annuity

Regular payments · constant or growing · present & future value

Read me first What is an annuity? ▾

What is an annuity?

  • A series of equal payments made at regular intervals (monthly, annually)
  • Examples: mortgage payments, pension payouts, loan repayments, savings plans

Growing Annuity

  • Payments grow at a constant rate g each period (e.g. salary rising 3% per year)
  • Requires r ≠ g
  • If r = g, PV = C × t / (1+r)

Key formulas at a glance

  • Annuity PV = C/r × [1 − (1+r)⁻ᵗ]
  • Annuity FV = C/r × [(1+r)ᵗ − 1]
  • Growing Annuity PV = C/(r−g) × [1 − ((1+g)/(1+r))ᵗ]
  • Perpetuity PV = C/r
  • Growing Perpetuity PV = C/(r−g)

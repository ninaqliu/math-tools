Addition & Subtraction

  • Fractions must share a common denominator — find the LCD first
  • Convert each fraction to the LCD, then add or subtract the numerators
  • The denominator never changes during the addition step

Multiplication

  • Multiply numerators together, then multiply denominators together
  • No common denominator needed — the simplest operation

Division — KCF method

  • Keep the first fraction · Change ÷ to × · Flip the second fraction
  • Then follow multiplication rules

Using the sliders

  • Adjust all four sliders (numerator and denominator of each fraction) from 1 to 12
  • The step-by-step working updates instantly as you move any slider

Fraction Operations

+   −   ×   ÷   fractions & mixed numbers

Read me first How each operation works ▾

Addition & Subtraction

  • Fractions must share a common denominator before you can add or subtract
  • Find the LCD (lowest common denominator), convert each fraction, then add/subtract numerators
  • The denominator never changes during the addition step

Multiplication

  • Multiply numerators together, then multiply denominators together
  • No common denominator needed — the simplest operation
  • Simplify (cross-cancel) before multiplying if possible

Division

  • Keep the first fraction, Change ÷ to ×, Flip the second fraction (reciprocal)
  • Then follow the multiplication steps
  • Memory trick: KCF — Keep, Change, Flip

Mixed numbers

  • Convert to improper fractions first, then apply the operation
  • Convert the result back to a mixed number at the end
